# AI Rocket + Astra Intelligence - Plans and Subscriptions

*Last Updated: December 2025*
*Status: Tentative and Subject to Change*

## Overview

AI Rocket + Astra Intelligence offers a tiered subscription model designed to serve users from individuals exploring AI capabilities to growing teams needing unlimited AI tools.

## Pricing Philosophy

**Unlimited Chat and Data for Everyone**: We believe every user should have full access to Astra's core intelligence from day one. Chat usage and data sync are unlimited across all plans because:

1. **Low Marginal Cost**: Modern AI APIs and storage make it economical to offer unlimited usage
2. **Maximum Engagement**: Users who chat frequently become highly engaged
3. **Natural Upgrade Path**: Users discover value faster and hit feature limits naturally

## Feature Glossary

### AI Specialists
Dedicated AI team members that handle specific business functions 24/7. Examples:
- Business Coach - Strategic guidance and goal tracking
- Finance Director - Budget analysis and financial insights
- Marketing Manager - Campaign ideas and content strategy
- Operations Lead - Process optimization and efficiency

### Team SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures)
AI-powered knowledge bases that document how your team operates:
- Document processes and workflows
- Create onboarding guides for new team members
- Maintain consistent procedures across your team
- Let Astra answer questions about "how we do things"

### Team Dashboards
Real-time, AI-updated displays showing the metrics that matter most:
- Customizable KPI tracking
- AI-generated insights and trends
- Automatic data visualization
- Team-wide visibility

### Team Agents
Custom AI assistants for specific tasks or areas:
- Task-specific (e.g., "Meeting Summarizer")
- Department-focused (e.g., "Sales Support Agent")
- Process-oriented (e.g., "Report Generator")

### Build with Astra
Create custom software applications with AI assistance:
- Describe what you want in plain English
- Astra generates functional applications
- No coding experience required
- Perfect for internal tools and utilities

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why are chat and data unlimited on the Free plan?**
A: We want you to fully experience Astra's intelligence before deciding on a plan. Unlimited access lets you discover value immediately without artificial barriers.

**Q: What happens after my 10-day trial?**
A: You choose your plan. If you don't upgrade, you'll be on the Free plan with limited visualizations and reports. All your data stays intact.

**Q: Can I switch plans later?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

**Q: What's the difference between Team Agents and AI Specialists?**
A: AI Specialists are role-based (like a Finance Director), while Team Agents are task-specific (like a Meeting Summarizer). Specialists have broader knowledge in their domain; Agents focus on specific workflows.

**Q: Is the Preview Pass price really locked forever?**
A: Yes. Preview Pass subscribers keep their $79/month rate regardless of future price increases.
